Introduction

Modern Nostalgia

Modern Nostalgia explores how modern interior design and architecture can draw inspiration from vintage aesthetics to blend classic elements with contemporary innovation. The magazine strives to highlight designers and architects who blend the aesthtics, explores the popularity of mid-century modern designs, and challenges of restoring historic building while updating for modern use.
